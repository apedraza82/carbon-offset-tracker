Methodology

How We Match Retirements to Firms

  • Data Sources: Retirement records from Verra (VCS), Gold Standard, American Carbon Registry (ACR), and Climate Action Reserve (CAR), sourced from the Berkeley Carbon Trading Project.
  • Beneficiary Extraction: Registry-specific parsing of retirement beneficiary fields, with fallback text extraction from notes and details fields.
  • Firm Matching: Two-stage process: (1) exact and normalized name matching against a cache of 3,600+ known beneficiary-to-firm mappings; (2) LLM-assisted entity resolution for new names, resolving subsidiaries and brands to parent listed firms.
  • Firm Universe: Publicly listed companies identified via FactSet entity IDs, covering ~50,000 global public firms.
  • Quality Control: LLM matches are assigned confidence scores (high/medium/low). Only high and medium matches are auto-accepted; low-confidence matches are flagged for manual review.
